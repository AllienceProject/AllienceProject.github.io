This project was developed under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. habil. Hein as part of the project management module.
Prof. Dr.-Ing. habil. Hein and Prof. Wurll jointly head the Robotics and Automation Laboratory. This was originally located in the K building of the university
and is now (summer semester 2021) moving to the new campus of the university (HKA 2030+/ TP). The new campus will thus be home to the new Master's programme
"Robotics and Artificial Intelligence in Production" and the Robotics and Automation Laboratory , which will enable students to learn and research in a practical way in the future.
